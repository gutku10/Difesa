A global movement for women achieving freedom to move without any fear.

THE REALITY

India was ranked first and named the world's most dangerous country for women in 2018. Fours years ago, in same survey, India was ranked fourth.

SOME FACTS

More than 90% cases about women rape, lost under the files due to society fear or lack of authority involvement and these women suffer for their whole life.

OUR IDEA

As per the present scenario it is very difficult to change the mindset of people.So we took a responsibility to bring a change by developing a gadget.

About our

We are a team of creative and passionate people driven by the idea that small efforts can lead to big changes. Makers, thinkers and explorers we strive to create meaningful digital products that connect with women, securing them in alarming situations.

With a vision to empower women and make them feel safer, we work with an indomitable zeal in an attempt to fuse creativity, innovation and technology together to bring in impact and extraordinary solutions.
